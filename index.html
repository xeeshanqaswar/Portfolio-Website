About me

My name is Zeeshan Qaswar and I'm a skilled Unity game developer with five years of experience. I'm passionate about creating immersive and engaging gaming experiences that captivate players of all ages. Over the years, I've honed my skills in game design, scripting, and optimization to bring my clients' visions to life.

I started my journey in game development by studying computer science and game design in university. After graduating, I landed my first job as a Unity developer and never looked back. Since then, I've had the privilege of working with a variety of clients and teams, developing games for mobile, PC, and console platforms.
